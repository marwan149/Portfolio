📖 MY STORY

My journey began in a small workshop where my father repaired old radios — I was mesmerized by the idea that invisible signals could bring a box to life. Years later, that curiosity evolved into a hunger for building intelligence. I wrote my first line of Python at 16, and the moment the terminal printed “Hello, world,” I knew code was my weapon.

The real forge was failure. My first Kaggle competition ended at the bottom 30%. But I studied winning solutions, slept little, and iterated. Six months later I grabbed my first silver medal, then gold. I realized that AI isn’t just algorithms — it’s a mindset: break down problems, experiment fearlessly, and never retreat.

Today, I stand at the intersection of software and hardware. I dream of autonomous systems that learn, adapt, and collaborate with humans. This portfolio isn’t a showcase of finished work — it’s a manifesto of battles fought and those yet to come. The code is never perfect; the champion is always in training.
